# **App Name**: Violet's Frenchies

## Core Features:

- Company Overview & Philosophy: A dedicated page introducing the breeding company, its commitment to ethical practices, and passion for French Bulldogs, with compelling photography.
- Available Puppies Showcase: A dynamic gallery displaying current puppies with high-resolution photos, individual bios, birthdates, and health information.
- Frenchie Photo Gallery: A beautifully curated photo gallery showcasing past litters, adult breeding dogs, and the daily life of French Bulldogs at Petit Paws.
- Puppy Adoption Guide: Detailed information about the adoption process, health guarantees, veterinary care, and tips for welcoming a new French Bulldog puppy.
- Inquiry & Contact Form: A simple and secure form allowing prospective buyers to easily send messages, ask questions, and express interest in available puppies.
- AI Puppy Name Generator: A generative AI tool that provides creative and unique name suggestions for new French Bulldog puppies, optionally based on user-provided themes or preferences.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color (buttons, headings): A sophisticated muted rose, evoking warmth and elegance. RGB: #844750.
- Background color: A very light, warm off-white with a hint of rose, providing a clean and inviting canvas. RGB: #F9F0F1.
- Accent color (highlights, interactive elements): A vibrant yet refined orchid pink, offering a gentle pop of color and contrast. RGB: #D07FCB.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif), chosen for its elegant and fashionable aesthetic, perfect for captivating titles.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif), providing a modern, readable, and warm feel for all descriptive text.
- Use clean, stylized vector icons; consider subtle paw print motifs or elegant French Bulldog silhouettes for navigation and feature indicators.
- Adopt a spacious and clean layout with ample whitespace to emphasize high-quality puppy photography and ensure readability. Implement a responsive grid for puppy listings.
- Incorporate subtle hover effects on interactive elements and smooth transitions for image galleries to enhance the user experience with gentle motion.
